Overview

LinkedIn is a professional networking platform designed to help individuals and organizations connect, share, and grow in their careers and industries. It enables users to build an online professional identity, engage with industry content, and explore career opportunities.

Getting Started

  1. Sign Up • Go to the Linkedin website{https://in.linkedin.com} or launch the LinkedIn mobile app.

    • Click or tap Join now

    • Enter your name, email address, and create a password.

    • Confirm your email address through the verification link LinkedIn sends to your inbox.

  2. Complete Your Setup

    • Add Your Location: Select your country and postal code to receive localized content and job suggestions.

    • Choose Job Preferences: Indicate your current job title, employment type, or if you're looking for a new role.

    • Upload a Profile Photo: Choose a clear, professional-looking headshot.
